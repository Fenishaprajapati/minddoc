So what is this phobia ?

Imagine your brain being a scaredy-cat on roller skates.
Phobia is like when that scaredy-cat sees something and freaks out so much
that it starts doing crazy tricks on its roller skates, like backflips and spins.


In a formal tone,
A phobia is a type of anxiety disorder characterized by an excessive and irrational
fear of specific objects, situations, or activities. Individuals with phobias experience
intense and persistent fear or anxiety when exposed to the feared stimulus, often
leading to avoidance behavior.
